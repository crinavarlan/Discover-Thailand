Islands in the Andaman Coast, Thailand

The Andaman coast is the west coast of Thailand on the south of the country, facing the Andaman sea. Most of the area belongs to the Krabi province. The main places to visit in the Andaman Coastare: BAMBOO ISLAND, KOH PHI PHI LEE, KOH PHI PHI DON, RAILAY, KOH LANTA, KOH PODA, KOH LIPE
